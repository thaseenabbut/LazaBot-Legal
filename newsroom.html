LazaBot Releases LU47.2: FlowControl Discontinued and Stability Improvements

To ensure peak performance and reliability during the development hiatus, LazaBot has released LU47.2, featuring the discontinuation of the underutilized FlowControl feature and several bug fixes. This update streamlines the bot, reducing potential instability while maintaining core functionality.

FlowControl, introduced in LU29, has been removed due to low adoption and maintenance challenges. Bug fixes include resolutions for /warn command crashes, improved AFK status auto-removal, enhanced webhook logging, and general performance optimizations for features like Event-Based AutoRoles and Advanced Suggestion System.
